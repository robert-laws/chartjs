Gem of the Week - Analysis

What's shown by the Gem of the Week Analytics is that GU-Q faculty, students, and staff found the Gems which offered something other than purely academic content. The most popular Gems were Press Reader, Online Documentaries, The New Look Research Guides (which is academic - but the gem focused on the guides as a whole), and What's New in the Library. While the purely academic Gems were viewed and do constitute important content to market, the Gems appear to be more effective if a mix of academic and popular-focused content is offered.
